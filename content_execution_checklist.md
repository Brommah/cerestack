## Emergency Response Playbook

### If Dragon 1 Goes Down
1. Immediate transparency tweet
2. Technical explanation within 1 hour
3. Regular updates until resolved
4. Post-mortem with improvements
5. Turn it into resilience proof point

### If Major FUD Emerges
1. Don't panic or over-respond
2. Address with data and dashboards
3. Show live network activity
4. Highlight production use cases
5. Let the community defend

### If Competitor Announces Similar Feature
1. Congratulate them publicly
2. Show our production metrics
3. Highlight unique advantages
4. Release new feature teaser
5. Stay focused on building
