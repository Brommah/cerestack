## How to Use These Materials

### For Recruiters
1. Post the enhanced job description on all major platforms
2. Use the technical details to screen candidates properly
3. Share production metrics to build credibility
4. Emphasize the ownership/revenue model as differentiator

### For Paid Advertising
1. Start with ads 1, 3, and 4 (money, tech, ownership)
2. Test 2-3 variants simultaneously 
3. Use provided targeting parameters
4. Track conversion by ad variant
5. Optimize based on quality of applications, not just quantity

### For Organic Outreach
1. DM strong candidates with personalized versions of the ads
2. Share in relevant Discord/Slack communities
3. Have current team members share with their networks
4. Post in Web3/AI job boards with full description

## Key Messaging Points

### What Makes Us Different
- **Production systems**: Not research, not testnet - real users, real revenue
- **Ownership model**: Engineers own their agents and earn from them
- **Technical depth**: 7-layer stack already processing millions of operations
- **Speed**: 60-minute deployment, not months of setup
- **Team**: Direct access to founders and architectural decisions

### Ideal Candidate Profile
- Has shipped AI products that generated revenue
- Comfortable with distributed systems
- Bias toward rapid prototyping
- Excited by ownership, not just salary
- Mission-aligned with sovereign AI

## Success Metrics

Track these to measure recruitment effectiveness:
1. **Application quality score** (technical depth of answers)
2. **Time to hire** (target: 7 days from first contact)
3. **Source effectiveness** (which ads/channels deliver best candidates)
4. **Culture fit rate** (% making it past mission alignment screen)
5. **Offer acceptance rate** (target: >80%)
