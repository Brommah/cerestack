## Campaign Notes

### Targeting Parameters
- **Interests**: LLMs, Computer Vision, MLOps, Distributed Systems, Web3
- **Job Titles**: AI Engineer, ML Engineer, AI Researcher, Staff/Senior Engineer
- **Companies**: FAANG, OpenAI, Anthropic, Databricks, Scale AI, Hugging Face
- **Locations**: SF Bay Area, NYC, London, Berlin, Remote
- **Seniority**: 3-10 years experience

### Best Practices
- Run A/B tests with different hooks (money vs tech vs ownership)
- Use eye-catching emojis sparingly (1-2 per ad)
- Test with and without specific salary ranges
- Retarget visitors who didn't complete applications
- Boost posts that get organic engagement

### Landing Page Must-Haves
- Application form above the fold
- "Apply in 5 minutes" promise
- Live counter of applications received
- Video of current team/culture
- Direct link to technical documentation

### Tracking
- UTM parameters for each ad variant
- Conversion tracking on application submit
- Time-on-page for quality scoring
- Source attribution in application form
