## Content Philosophy

### The Three Commandments
1. **Show, Don't Tell** - Every claim needs a dashboard, demo, or deployed example
2. **Weaponize the Vision** - Content drives specific actions: deploy or invest
3. **One Truth, Two Dialects** - Same product, different languages for different audiences

### Fred's Rules
- "No content without proof"
- "No education without activation"  
- "No community building without revenue generation"
- "Execute with violent urgency"
